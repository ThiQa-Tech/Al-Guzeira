Al-Guzira States

Al-Guezira Estates is a residential community of luxury classic indexs in West Cairo’s Sheikh Zayed City. The community features beautifully designed villas, twin houses, and a first of its kind Clubhouse which includes Saffron Restaurant, a full-scale gym, fitness and aerobics facilities, and swimming pools. The stunning landscaped gardens are meticulously manicured all year round. Al-Guezira Estates has hosted countless celebrations, weddings, and birthday parties made possible by the availability of the entertainment amenities. In a nutshell, Al-Guezira Estates offers a lavish lifestyle, a serene and secure environment, and the absolute pinnacle of suburban living in Egypt.
